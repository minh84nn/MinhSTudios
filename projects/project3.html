Sunset Supper

Project Description
Poster Design for Pike Market Place in Seattle, WA. It is a market for fresh seafood and produce. A gathering of people for a community supper. I used the colors of the sunset and the venue scene for the fish scales as plates. It is a supper event so I used plates to represent this topic.
