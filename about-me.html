Biography
Minh Nguyen is a graphic designer with 8 years professional experience with a focus in print, illustration, logo and web design. He also has experience with Wordpress, Bootstrap, photography, drawing, and painting. Minh aims to have a continued advancement in web development as part of his goal set. Born in Vietnam and raised in Austin (Texas), he attended Texas State University, gaining his Bachelors of Fine Arts in (2010). After completing an internship in San Antonio (Clockwork Studios), Minh began working in-house for a Real Estate firm where he was immersed in website design, real estate photography, print, and direct mail. He soon became a marketing coordinator for Newmark Knight Frank and works with reputable clients to market their commercial real estate assets. He is responsible for the day-to-day production of proposals, offering memorandums, marketing collateral, flyers, marketing videos, and research reports. With a passion to always grow and become a better designer, he is open and available for any multi-faceted project needs.
